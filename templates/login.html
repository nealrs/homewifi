FAQ & Privacy Policy

What does Home WiFi do?

Home WiFi is an Alexa Skill that learns and remembers your WiFi password, so you don't have to.

How does it actually do that?

1. Log in above to enter your WiFi network name and password.

2. Enable our Alexa skill and link it to your Home WiFi account using your Amazon account.

3. Say "Alexa, open Home WiFi" and listen as Alexa spells out your wireless network name and password for visitors. You can also access the information in your Alexa smartphone app.
